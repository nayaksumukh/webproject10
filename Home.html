What is Astrology?

Astrology holds a significant place in Hindu culture, offering profound insights into life's journey and influencing important decisions. Rooted in ancient Vedic traditions, Hindu astrology, or "Jyotish Shastra," interprets celestial movements and their impact on human lives. This cosmic science goes beyond mere horoscope readings, forming an integral part of Hindu beliefs and practices.



Why astrology should be preferred?

Astrology can offer predictions about the future. Astrologers also believe that the position of the planets can be used to predict future events, such as career changes, relationship challenges, and health problems. This can be helpful for people who are trying to plan ahead or who are looking for reassurance about the future.



How can Astrology help me in future?

It can provide insights into personality and behavior. Astrologers believe that the position of the planets at the time of birth can influence a person's personality, strengths, weaknesses, and life path. This can be helpful for people who are trying to understand themselves better or who are looking for guidance in making decisions.
